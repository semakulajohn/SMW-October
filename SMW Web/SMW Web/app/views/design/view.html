Design

Our motivation is to change the way the world sees you by creating your dream home. It’s one thing to create stunning design; it’s truly exceptional design that engages the end user and creates a response.

For our client; we make sure we understand and we deliver affective solutions. Our minds are as sharp as they are passionate. One conversation can lead to many possibilities.

SMW provide multi-trade contruction services to new build and refurbishment projects. Our work is focused on the alteration and fit out of existing buildings as well as carrying out new build and extension projects direct for the client.

We specialise in working closely with the client to take any project through from design conception to completion, advising at every stage on cost viability, alternative construction, finishing methods, and always mindful of the overall programme.

We are keen to involve all parties on projects as early as possible.

SMW offers a range of services which include
  • Planning
  • Architectural design
  • Structural design
  • Mechanical & electrical works
  • All types of roofing
  • Decorations
  • Landscaping & gardening
  • Specialist finishings
  • After sales service and maintenance
