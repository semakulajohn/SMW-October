Welcome to SMW

We are a small building construction company catering for customers based in both Uganda and Diaspora.

We specialise in all aspects of building construction including new domestic and commercial builds, conversions, extensions and refurbishment.

As part of our construction service, we offer full building design service to our clients including, land surveying, architectural, structural, electrical, plumbing and mechanical services.

In addition, SMW takes care of planning application issues associated with new builds.

We also manufacture concrete products such as, culvets, paving products, etc under our parent company Mutec Construction, which has been operating since 1950.

Well-maintained building or house creates the right impression, it attracts the right and high paying tenants and reduces operational costs. SMW provide maintenance service solutions tailed to suit clients' needs such as; holiday homes, rentals, commercial premises, etc.

We take great pride in our reputation for customer satisfaction, delivering a high quality finished product on time and to budget. Our business is built on the recommendations and referrals of our past clients.

Our Company Objectives

SMW aims to deliver outstanding results through effective practices and good work ethics, allowing us to meet and exceed client expectations.

We seek to cultivate strong, long term working relationships built on mutual respect.

To enable our business to grow and to improve the service we offer, we continually strive to up-skill and innovate.
